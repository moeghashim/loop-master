The full system (converged)

GitHub is the source of truth. A dispatcher Action decides the cast and the solo/reviewed shape and writes labels — it never runs an agent. Execution happens in the vendor apps (where you can jump in); CI is the gate; you merge. After merge, the nightly grader turns outcomes into the routing that shapes your next cast.
