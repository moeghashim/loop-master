1 · Per-issue flow — the two gates

Mechanical gate first (a hard floor), then the AI sign-off on top of it, then your merge. A red gate or an unapproved review sends work back to the executor. 

flowchart TD
  P["Plan
planner agent"]:::agent --> X["Execute
executor agent"]:::agent
  X --> V{"make verify
mechanical floor"}:::mech
  V -->|"non-zero"| FB["Fix or block
re-run, or block"]:::fail
  FB -.->|"after fix"| X
  V -->|"exit 0"| R{"Review sign-off
reviewer agent"}:::ai
  R -->|"not approved"| RC["Request changes"]:::ai
  RC -.->|"back to executor"| X
  R -->|"approved"| PR["Open PR
board to Review"]:::auto
  PR --> M["You merge
board to Done"]:::human

2 · Measurement & routing loop

Triage picks the cast from the routing table (mostly best-fit, sometimes explore). After merge, a blinded grader scores the work; ratings feed back into the table, keyed by agent × role × work-type. 

flowchart TD
  RT[("Routing table
best agent per role")]:::data --> TR["Triage: pick cast
plus occasional explore"]:::human
  TR --> PL["Run pipeline
plan to exec to review to merge"]:::agent
  PL --> GR{"Grader
blinded, independent"}:::ai
  GR --> RA[("Ratings
agent, role, work-type")]:::data
  RA -.->|"feeds next triage"| RT
